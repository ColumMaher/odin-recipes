Description

Lasagna is a delicious and hearty Italian dish made of stacked layers of wide pasta sheets, typically layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy b√©chamel sauce, and flavorful fillings such as ground meat, cheese, and vegetables. The layers are then baked until the pasta becomes tender, and the flavors meld together to create a scrumptious, comforting casserole. It is a popular and satisfying meal enjoyed by people around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Italian sausage and 2lb oflean ground beef
  • 1 onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • parsley, basil, salt, pepper
  • fennel seeds
  • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  • 3 eggs

Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
